Tebra is launching two new AI tools designed to address administrative burdens in independent practices. The AI Billing Assistant flags high-risk claims before submission to reduce denials, while the AI Voice Assistant answers patient calls instantly and routes requests directly into the EHR system. Both tools are built natively within Tebra's existing workflows, requiring no third-party add-ons or new systems.
